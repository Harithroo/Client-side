Description

A divers paradise in the Baie du Marin, a legendary spot. Its bungalows are discreetly lodged in a tropical garden beside the white sand beach in superb Marin Bay. A magical site where you can enjoy a taste of everything, alone or with family or friends. Try water sports and the magnificent Club Med Spa*. You will be enchanted by the exotic flavours of the local cuisine and the joyful spirit of the Caribbean.
